## Net effect
The decision-tree editor's graph drives customer-facing walks end-to-end. Every branch routes correctly server-side; every per-step answer + contact data persists to `survey_responses` and `customers`. Form builder accepts every default config without validator errors. QR codes are first-class managed with click tracking. Onboarding gates on review-platform URLs and offers Places API auto-resolve.
